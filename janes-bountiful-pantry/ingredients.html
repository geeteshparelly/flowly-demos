Our Flour Blend

Getting the right texture without gluten took years of testing. Most gluten-free baked goods are either gummy, crumbly, or taste like cardboard. Ours don't — here's what we use:

Certified Gluten-Free Ingredients

  • Oat Flour Certified GF oats — adds softness and mild flavor
  • Almond Flour Adds moisture and richness without dairy
  • Tapioca Starch Creates chewiness and helps bind everything together

All our flours are certified gluten-free and sourced from suppliers who test for cross-contamination. We don't just trust labels — we verify.

Allergen Questions

Is your kitchen certified gluten-free?

Our kitchen is dedicated gluten-free — no wheat, barley, or rye is ever present. While we're not third-party certified, we follow strict protocols and use only certified GF ingredients.

I have a severe nut allergy. Are any products nut-free?

Unfortunately, no. All our products contain almond flour, and our kitchen processes multiple tree nuts. We cannot guarantee nut-free products at this time.

What about soy?

Our chocolate chip cookie dough may contain traces of soy from the chocolate chips. All other products are soy-free.

Is everything vegan?

Yes. No eggs, dairy, honey, or animal products in any of our items. We use flax eggs for binding and coconut oil instead of butter.

Can I request a custom item for my allergy?

Reach out and we'll see what we can do. Some modifications are possible depending on the specific allergy.
