Common Questions

For full allergen details, see our ingredients page.

Is everything really vegan?
Yes. No eggs, no dairy, no honey, no animal products. Every item we make.
Is your kitchen gluten-free?
Yes. We bake in a dedicated gluten-free space. No wheat, barley, or rye ever enters our kitchen.
What about nut allergies?
Some products contain tree nuts (pecans, pistachios, almonds). Check individual items or contact us.
How do I pay?
Payment at pickup. We accept Square (card), cash, and Venmo.
