Allergen FAQ

Your safety and peace of mind come first

Are all your products vegan?
Yes! Every single item we make is 100% vegan. No eggs, no dairy, no honey, no animal products whatsoever.
Is your kitchen gluten-free?
Yes! We bake in a dedicated gluten-free environment. There is no wheat, barley, or rye in our kitchen ‚Äî ever. All our flour blends are certified gluten-free.
What flour do you use?
We use a custom blend of certified gluten-free oat flour, almond flour, and tapioca starch. We're happy to share specific ingredients for any product ‚Äî just ask!
Are your products dairy-free?
Absolutely. We never use milk, butter, cream, or any dairy derivatives. We use coconut oil and plant-based alternatives instead.
Do you use eggs?
No eggs! We use flax eggs and aquafaba (chickpea water) as binding agents. Safe for vegans and those with egg allergies.
What about nut allergies?
Some of our products contain tree nuts (pecans, pistachios, almonds). If you have a nut allergy, please check individual product ingredients or contact us.
Are your products certified?
Our flour blends are certified gluten-free. While we're not a certified facility, we maintain strict protocols and bake exclusively gluten-free, vegan products.
